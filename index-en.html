Game modes

5 ways to play with movies & shows

Quiz, odd-one-out, screenshots, actors to connect… each game tests your pop culture knowledge in a different way.

Mode 01
The Classic

One question, four answers. Simple, fast, effective.

Mode 02
The Odd One Out

Six options, one doesn't belong. Can you spot the impostor?

Mode 03
Freeze Frame

Up to 6 screenshots to identify the movie. Be the fastest.

Mode 04
Focus

The image sharpens gradually. Answer fast to score big.

Mode 05
6 Degrees

Connect two actors through others in less than 6 steps. A unique mode for the true fans.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the app already available?

Absolute Popcorn is available on iOS and Android. Download links will be added as soon as the app is live.

Do I need to be a movie buff to enjoy it?

Not at all. The app is designed for everyone: movie fans, series addicts, casual viewers, families and groups of friends. If you've ever watched a film, you're ready to play.

Is it free to play?

The app is free to download. Optional in-app purchases are available to enhance your experience, including an ad-free mode.

How many languages are available?

8 languages are available at launch. More may be added based on community feedback.

Who makes Absolute Popcorn?

Absolute Popcorn is developed by Timing Corporation, a French company founded in 2025. For any questions, reach us at contact@absolute-popcorn.com.
